About Me

My name is Rebeccah, and I'm from the metro-Detroit area in Michigan. With a background as a Registered Dietitian, I have spent years working closely with patients to help them achieve better health outcomes. Driven by a passion for improving healthcare, I’m now excited to transition into software development, where I can merge my clinical expertise and growing technical skills to create innovative tech solutions that make a meaningful impact on the healthcare system.

Outside of my professional life, I have a variety of interests that keep me inspired. I love spending time with loved ones, whether it’s exploring the outdoors or sharing a meal together. Running, weight lifting, and regular walks with my dog, Oscar, are essential parts of my routine. I also enjoy video games, building LEGO sets, and trying new recipes in the kitchen.
